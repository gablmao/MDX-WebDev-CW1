100 years has long passed since the last time rats have infested your town. A group of brave cats, more known as the Purr-ice Force, Fought back the rat attack and eventually came victorious. Since then, everything was at peace and harmony...

After a hard day's work of slightly irritating your owners, scratching furniture for no particular reason and meowing loudly at unreasonable times, you go for your well-deserved rest.

Until...

You were suddenly woken up by the sound of chaos outside your cheeky British house.

HOLY WACKAMOLY! Rats Everywhere! A Rat Infestation! And not just the typical normal house rats this time...

Neighbourhood's favourite cat!

You, a seemingly looking normal orange tabby cat actually born with improved abilities than other cats, have to save your neighbourhood from being taken over by rats.

This time however, the rats came back with upgrades.

Could your enhanced predatory abilities keep up with the rats? Dodge their attacks and claw them out of your streets! Before you get overwhelmed... and leave your home in ruin!

RATS!
